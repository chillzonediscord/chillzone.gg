Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

ChillZone ("we," "us," "our") operates the Chill Guy Discord bot ("the Bot"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data from Discord:

  • User ID: Your Discord user ID to identify your account and associate your virtual pets, balance, and activity.
  • Server ID: The Discord server ID where you interact with the Bot.
  • Message content: Only messages that begin with the Bot's command prefix are processed. We do not read or store general chat messages.
  • Voice channel activity: Join/leave times and channel IDs for voice channel tracking features. We do not record audio.

Data we generate:

  • Virtual pets: Pet data (species, images, breeding history) associated with your user ID.
  • Transaction history: Records of in-game purchases, trades, and currency changes.
  • Reports: If you submit a report, the report content and attached evidence are stored until resolved.

Data from payments:

  • Payments are processed by Discord or PayPal. We do not store credit card numbers, billing addresses, or other payment details. We receive only a transaction confirmation and your user ID.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide Bot features (pets, trading, voice tracking, moderation)
  • To process purchases and deliver virtual items
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and Terms of Service violations
  • To improve the Bot and its features

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on a secure server. Pet images are stored in Amazon S3. We retain your data as long as your account is active within the ChillZone ecosystem.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Discord: As required by Discord's API to provide Bot functionality.
  • Payment processors: Discord and PayPal, only to process your purchases.
  • Cloud services: Amazon Web Services (S3) for image storage.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your data through Bot commands (e.g., viewing your pets, receipts, and stats).
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your data by contacting us. Note that deleting your data will permanently remove all your virtual pets, balance, and history.
  • Portability: You may request an export of your data by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

The Bot is available to Discord users who meet Discord's minimum age requirement (13 years, or higher in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children below this age.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encrypted database connections and access controls. However, no system is perfectly secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be announced in our Discord server. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, join our Discord server at discord.gg/chillzone or email privacy@chillzone.gg.
